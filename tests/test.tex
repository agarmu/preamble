\section{Math}
Let $A \subseteq \RR$ with $|A| = n$. Then $\poly(n) = n^{O(1)}$ and $\polylog(n) = (\log n)^{O(1)}$. Also $\emptyset \neq \zo$ and $\zon$.

Expectation operator: $\ep_{x\sim \Unif([n])}[x] = (n+1)/2$ with $\EE[\ind\{X>0\}]$ well-defined.

Operators: $\im(f)$, $\diam(S)$, $\Sigma = \sum$, $\phi, \epsilon, \eps$ are curly.

Delimiters: $\parof{x+y}$, $\braof{x+y}$, $\setof{1,2,3}$, $\ceilof{x}$, $\floorof{x}$, $\angof{x}$.
